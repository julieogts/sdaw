Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

At Sanrico Mercantile Inc., we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide and data obtained automatically when using our website:

Information You Provide:

  • Account details (name, email, phone, password)
  • Profile information (addresses, preferences)
  • Order details and payment information
  • Communications and feedback

Automatic Collection:

  • Device information (IP address, browser type)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time on site)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

Purpose Legal Basis
Process orders and payments Contract fulfillment
Provide customer support Legitimate interest
Improve website and services Legitimate interest
Prevent fraud and ensure security Legal obligation

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with:

Service Providers:

  • Payment processors for secure transactions
  • Shipping companies for delivery
  • IT services for website maintenance

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by law
  • To protect rights and safety
  • In response to legal requests

4. Data Security and Rights

Security Measures: We use encryption, secure servers, and regular audits to protect your data. All payments processed through PCI DSS compliant providers.

Your Rights:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (subject to legal requirements)
  • Object to certain data uses
Contact us to exercise these rights. We respond within 30 days.

5. Contact and Updates

Cookies: We use essential, performance, and functional cookies. You can control cookies through browser settings.

Data Retention: We keep information as long as needed for services, legal compliance, and business purposes.
