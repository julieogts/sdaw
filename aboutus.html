About Us

Sanrico Mercantile, Inc. is a trusted, family-owned hardware store located in Regalado, Fairview, Quezon City. Established in May 2003, our store was founded to supply high-quality construction materials for our parent company, Ricsons Corporation. Through the collective investment of five relatives, Sanrico has grown into a reliable source for contractors, builders, and homeowners, offering a wide range of tools and supplies to meet diverse project needs.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction drives everything we do. Whether you're tackling a large-scale construction project or a home improvement task, Sanrico Mercantile is here to provide the materials and support you need to get the job done.
